I work across both sides of security — breaking into systems to find what an attacker would find, and investigating incidents after the fact to reconstruct exactly what happened. That combination of offensive testing and forensic investigation is unusual, and it's the core of how I work.

My background spans vulnerability assessment and penetration testing, digital forensics and cyber crime investigation, SOC monitoring with Splunk, and independent research into malware and emerging threats.
